Our Approach

Drawing inspiration from the Finnish system, Reggio Emilia and the British Early Years Foundation Stages curriculum, our eclectic approach combines global best practices to create an enriching learning experience for our students. VLC maintains an individualized, child-led, experiential, multi-sensory approach to learning, enabling students to absorb and practically apply what they are taught.

We Teach EQ

The early years are critical for the development of healthy emotional and social skills. Real-life skills help children understand themselves better. Setting and cleaning up toys, helping prepare for community snack time, caring for art materials and similar everyday tasks help children feel a sense of ownership of their school, their community, their world and their life.

Everyday Outside

We believe time spent outside is time well spent. We know that it sharpens kids intellectually, emotionally and physically and reduces anxiety and stress. Exposure to nature is an important feature of life at VLC. Our nature immersion program encourages nature exploration throughout the year and every morning starts with learning through play.

Experiential Learning

True experiential learning is not just hands on, but is also reflective and learner-centric. VLC students are encouraged to engage with the interdisciplinary curriculum through projects based around art, music, cooking, technology, mechanics, carpentry, and gardening. They then reflect on their findings in group discussions, peer presentations and learning journals. This immersive approach facilitates the development of new perspectives and higher order thinking.

Personal Goals

Despite our challenging and rigorous curriculum at VLC, our perspective on achievement is quite encompassing: We believe in personal goals and competition only with oneself. The term report cards are structured as indications of a learning journey rather than formal grades. We teach our students that we hold them in high esteem and that positive self-belief, coupled with our intellectually enriching curriculum, creates an exciting atmosphere of learning as a driven community.
